We are proud to call North Carolina home.

Most of our families came here as immigrants, and today’s immigrants come here for the same reasons – for safety, education or simply a job that lets them feed their children.

Uniting NC works to make North Carolina a place in which all people, including immigrants, have the opportunity to thrive and to engage in their communities. By creating communities that bring out the best in people, we are all better off.

Our goal is to make our state a place in which all North Carolinians feel welcome, and together expand prosperity and well-being for all.
