You’re invited to a free film screening

Please join Uniting NC and USCRI North Carolina for a free screening of Welcome to Shelbyville, the newest documentary from the company that brought us such great films as Food, Inc. and The Visitor. You won’t see this powerful work anywhere else in the Triangle, because we have exclusive rights to the first screening. RSVP at the bottom of this page.

Date: Wednesday, October 27
Time: 7 p.m. (Please arrive a bit early to say hello to us, buy refreshments and find your seat.)
Venue: Galaxy Cinema
Address: 770 Cary Towne Boulevard, Cary, North Carolina
Cost: FREE

Welcome to Shelbyville explores a small Tennessee community’s struggle to deal with an influx of immigrants and refugees from around the world. Its subject matter is extremely relevant to North Carolina. Watch the film trailer here. After the film, we’ll talk about how we can make our state a more welcoming place for refugees and immigrants.

The screening will be held at the Galaxy Cinema in Cary, a lovely theater with plenty of parking and a lobby where you can buy a glass of wine or a soft drink and mingle a bit before or after the film. There is no charge to see the film, but we hope you’ll consider making a donation to further Uniting NC’s work. It’s going to be a great evening with a diverse crowd, and we hope to see you there.
